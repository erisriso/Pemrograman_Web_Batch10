5 Jenis Operator

  1. Operator Aritmatika
    1. + Additonal --> Penambahan
    2. - Substraction --> pengurangan
    3. * Multiplication --> Perkalian
    4. / Division --> Pembagian
    5. ++ Increment (penambahan dengan kelipatan)
    6. -- Decrement (pengurangan dengan kelipatan)
    7. % Modulus --> sisa hasil bagi
  2. Operator Pembanding (Comparison Operator)
    1. == Equal (Sama dengan)
    2. != Not Equal (Tidak Sama Dengan)
    3. > Greater than (lebih besar dari)
    4. < Less Than (Lebih kecil dari)
    5. >= Greater Than Or Equal To (Lebih Besar Sama Dengan)
    6. <= Less Than Or Equal To (Lebih Kecil Sama Dengan)
  3. Logical/Relational Operator
    1. && And
    2. || Or
    3. ! Not
    4. Bitwise
      1. & Bitwise And
      2. | Bitwise Or
      3. ^ Bitwise Not
  4. Assigment Operator (Operator Penugasan)
    1. = Simple Assignment
    2. += Add dan Assigment
    3. -= Substract and Assigment
    4. /= Divide and Assigment
    5. %== Modulus and Assigment
  5. Conditional Operator
    1. ?: Conditional
    2. typeof operator (string, number, boolean, object, function, undefined, Null)
